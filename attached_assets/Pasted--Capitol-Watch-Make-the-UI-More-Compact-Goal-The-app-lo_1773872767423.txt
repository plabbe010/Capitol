# Capitol Watch — Make the UI More Compact

## Goal
The app looks great but takes up too much vertical space. Make everything denser
so users can see more trades without scrolling. Think of how Bloomberg or a
trading terminal uses space — tight rows, smaller text, less padding everywhere.

---

## Specific changes to make

### Stats bar (the row with 150, 125, 30, 120, 0)
- Reduce the number height from ~40px to around 28px font size
- Reduce padding inside each stat cell from ~16px to 10px top/bottom
- Make the label text 11px and the sub-label 11px (they can be on the same line,
  separated by a · if needed)
- Example target: `150  all disclosures` on two lines but tight, not airy

### Trade cards
This is the biggest win. Currently each card is too tall.
- Reduce card padding from ~16px to 10px vertical, 14px horizontal
- Reduce margin between cards from 8px to 4px
- Make the member name 13px instead of 14-15px
- Make the asset/ticker line 12px
- Make the amount and days-ago text 11px
- The ticker chip (MSFT, META square) — reduce from 44px to 36px
- Put the Buy/Sell badge, amount, and days-ago all on ONE line on the right side
  instead of stacking them vertically

### The HIGH ALERT label strip above each card
- Currently it sits above the card as a separate line, adding height
- Move it INSIDE the card as the first line, in a smaller font (11px)
- This removes the gap between the label and the card

### Filter row
- The two rows of filters (search + party/type/chamber on one row, then
  FILTER: All trades / Alerts only / Flagged on a second row) — combine into
  one row if possible, or reduce vertical padding on the filter container
- Reduce filter button padding to 4px 10px

### Section spacing
- Reduce the gap between the filter section and the first trade card from ~20px to 8px
- Reduce padding inside the AI summary card

### Tab bar
- Reduce tab padding from 12px to 8px top/bottom

---

## What NOT to change
- Colors, fonts, or the overall visual identity
- The flagging logic or data
- The two-tier alert/flag system
- Any functionality (watchlist, filters, AI signals, expand on click)
- The header

---

## Target feel
Each trade card should be about 60-70px tall in its collapsed state (currently
feels like ~90-100px). The whole feed should feel like a dense, scannable list
— more like a news ticker or a trading app, less like a card-based mobile app.
